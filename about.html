Our Mission

OSTA Trading GmbH is a specialised distributor of active pharmaceutical ingredients, excipients and chemicals for food, cosmetics industries. We supply products of European manufacturers as well as products from all around the globe. Our goal is to provide customers with efficient and high quality services, meet delivery deadlines and guarantee compliance with all modern requirements for the distribution of ingredients for various industries. Our team of experienced and qualified professionals works every day to satisfy your most demanding needs.
